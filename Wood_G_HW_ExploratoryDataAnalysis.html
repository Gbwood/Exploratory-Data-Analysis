2. What types of data transformation did you have to do for correlation analysis?¶

To transform the data, Yes and No were changed to 1 and 0, Gender was broken into categories and Marital status was changed into dummy variables.

3. Explain the results of the correlation analysis?¶

The strongest correlation was between weight and having a second heart attack. Those who weighed more had a higher chance of having a second heart attack.There is also a correlation between getting a divorce and having a second heart attack.

4. Describe the characteristics of people who are at risk of having 2nd heart attack.¶

People who are more at risk of having a second heart attack are

  • older people
  • overweight
  • divorced
  • high cholesterol

5. What is your general recommendation for the healthcare provider?¶

To help prevent people having 2nd heart attacks, a hospital should encourage it's clients to lose weight, avoid divorce(?!) and keep cholesterol levels at a healthy level
